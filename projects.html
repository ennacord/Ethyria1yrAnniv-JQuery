Project: CONNECT



This project started back in February and it's just an idea dedicated to Enna, this song is chosen because the lyrics are very applicable to us Aloupeeps and Enna. Our goal is to have Aloupeeps sing it in their respective languages and to show that we Aloupeeps are distributed all around the world and how Enna connected us together with her beautiful voice. So for the teaser part, as this is Halloween month, we tried to make the teaser a bit creepy (I have no idea if that worked or not) and to also show some artwork that is in the MV.

Enna has grown so much from her comfort zone of being an introvert in the past year and that itself is a huge accomplishment and we're so proud of her. Enna believe it or not has helped many people either through streams or her jokes to be more positive about life, she also has heavenly vocals that bless our ears every time she sings! Enough said by us, we thank Ethyria for the amazing year they have provided to us and we Enna's Angelic Teahouse wish Ethyria a Happy Anniversary!
