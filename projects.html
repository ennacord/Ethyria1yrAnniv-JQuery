Wish of this Songbird
[Marvin's HEAVEN'S RAVE PARTY Remix!]
orig. Enna Alouette


DISCLAIMER: Please, if you play this on stream, do not share the audio.



Description:

HAPPY 1 YEAR ANNIVERSARY ENNA! This is a small personal project that started as just some fooling around, but became one of my biggest learning moments, passions and works this year! I wanna share it with and give back to the wonderful community you created, and I hope I have been able to do justice to your wonderful first original song! The aloupeeps will wait patiently for the next time the Bird of Heaven graces our ears, be it during chaotic and heartwarming streams, or when your fair and powerful notes reverberate through the skies!

When listening to the original after a while, I knew the first orchestral section into the choir HAD to get a Psytrance twist! Aaaaaaand when I'd least expected I had added another 7 minutes to it, cause it lived rent free in my head. It has elements from Psytrance, Frenchcore, Deep House, Pop, Happy Hardcore, J-Core and DnB! With every section and drop I wanted to challenge the expectations and my technique a bit more, all while maintaining the original's tone of being epic, orchestral, mellow, chaotic, ever-so-hellish, divine and plot-driven. A bit of a story plays in my head when I listen back to it, maybe Enna making her way back to heaven after soothing the souls and mending the hearts.
